## Browser-Tested User Intent Paths

- Open app and verify product identity.
- Record Presence and verify local receipt/no gamification.
- Open optional Gratitude prompt.
- Open Guide Me and selected-practice ELI10.
- Click count cards for practices, scores/streaks, and innovations.
- Filter idea rows and open an idea detail.
- Open local report and markdown export.
- Stage Elaine local handover gate.
- Run synthetic-data boundary/status/cleanup verification.
- Open all Evidence summary buttons.
- Open RAG / Sources, stage a source reference, and search local RAG/source material.
- Open every Life Manager v2 menu group and screen.
- Verify Home, daily flow, guidance, private, plans, knowledge, reviews, and Admin / Proof screens are tabbed and do not require middle-column scrolling.
- Click v2 workflow buttons and verify local receipt endpoints instead of external sends/writes.
- Open n8n workflow pack proof and verify four disabled workflow imports plus dry-run/preflight/live-gate receipts.
- Open Academy proof and verify programmes, lessons, practice receipt, and readiness.
- Open PIN Strategist proof and verify decision brief, people, sources, radar, questions, queue, review, and local receipts.
- Open Cross-App Packets and verify receiver rules, packet preview, local receipt, and no-silent-send boundary.
- Open Cross-App Packets -> Automation and verify policy, three automated event runs, linked local packet receipts, and no-silent-send boundary.
- Open live-action status and verify missing credentials/approval produce exact local blocker receipts.

## Remaining Partial Or Blocked Behaviours

None for the approved full local/internal CK Life OS scope. External production sends/writes/deploy/publish, live voice transcription, Ripple calendar writes, cross-device memory sync, n8n live import/execution, and paid provider execution now have CK-owned disabled n8n preflights plus callable approval gates with exact local blocker receipts. PIN Strategist and Cross-App Packets are local-only and do not require provider calls. Cross-App Automation automatically creates local packet receipts only; actual live execution or sibling-app writes remain credential/endpoint/receiver-adapter/approval dependent and are not claimed unless those prerequisites are present.
